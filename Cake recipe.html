Strawberry shortcake

Ingredients

  • Flour 500g
  • Eggs 10
  • Sugar 50g
  • Butter 100g
  • Strawberries 20
  • Whipping cream 3cups
  • Baking powder 1tbsp

Instructions

Mix baking powder and flour well. Then add butter and beat well. Then add sugar and beat. Then add eggs one by one while beating.

Take 10 strawberries. Cut in half.

Now take a baking tray. Pour the cake mixer into the tray. Sprade strawberry halfs all over. Then bake it for 40 mins.
