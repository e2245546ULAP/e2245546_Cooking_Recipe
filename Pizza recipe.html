Pepperoni pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour 300g
  • Yeast 1tbsp
  • Warm water 100ml
  • Mozzarella cheese 50g
  • Packet of pepperoni
  • Tomato paste
  • Chopped Onions
    • li>Chilli flakes 1tbsp

Instructions

Add a little bit of yeast to flour and keep it for 1 hour. Then slowly add hot water and mix the flour utill it becomes unsticky. Then make a round.

Then apply a layer of tomato paste. Add chooped onions on top of it. Sprinkle some chilli flakes. Then add the pepperoni slices. Add cheese on top.

Bake for 20 mins untill it is golden and crunchy. Serve hot.
