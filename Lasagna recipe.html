Chicken Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles 5
  • Chicken breasts 2
  • Cream cheese 50g
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt 1tbsp
  • Chilli flakes 1tbsp
  • water 100ml

Instructions

Cook the lasagna noodles in boiling water. Add salt as taste to the boiling water.

We are going to make the chicken sauce.Get a pan and add 2 chicken breastrs. Then add tomato paste. Add some chilli flakes. Add little bit of water and let it cook for 10 mins.

Now get a baking tray. Put a lasagna noodle at the bottom. Next add a layer of the chicken sauce. Put a lasagna noodle. Then a layer of cream cheese. A lasagna noodle. Again layer a layer of chicken sauce. Lasagna noodle.Then a layer of cram cheese. And finally a lasagna noodle on top.

Bake it for 30 mins untill the lasagna becoms golden color.
