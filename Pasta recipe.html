Cheezy beefy Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta 300g
  • Beef 200g
  • Cheddar 50g
  • Mozzarella 50g
  • American cheese 50g
  • Olive oil 2tbsp
  • Chopped Onions
  • Salt 1tbsp
    • li>Chilli flakes 1tbsp
  • Small bunch of parsley
  • Water 100ml

Instructions

Boil the water and add the pasta to the boiling water. Cook for 2 mins and then Add the beef. Add salt as taste. Cook for 5 mins.Then drain the remaning water out.

Get a pan and add 1tbsp of olive oil. Then add chopped onions. Add 1 tbsp of chilli flakes. Then add all the cheese. Cheddar, american and then mozzarella. After the cheese got melted add the pre cooked pasta and the beef. Cook for 5 mins. Add some parsley on top and serve straight from the pan.
